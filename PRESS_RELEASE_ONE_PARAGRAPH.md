# UrbanPulse Launch – One-Paragraph Version

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Berlin, December 27, 2025** – SkyMind today launched **UrbanPulse**, a free research platform providing access to ten years (2015–2024) of verified demographic and housing market data across 15 global cities, with Berlin as the flagship dataset featuring district-level heatmaps for over 200 neighborhoods. Data are compiled from Eurostat, Destatis, and national statistical agencies; the platform presents observable correlations between demographic shifts and housing market changes, emphasizing that these are correlations, not causal claims, with full methodology and confidence intervals available online. Basic exploration is free without registration; advanced exports and API access are available on request. The platform is GDPR-compliant and now live worldwide. For screenshots, data requests, or interviews contact info@sky-mind.com.
